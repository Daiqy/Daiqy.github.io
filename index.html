Research

I am passionate about 3D Vision and Computer Graphics. My current interest lies in exploring neural scene representations to effectively capture the visual, geometric, and physical properties of our surrounding, enabling accurate reconstruction and flexible interaction in virtual worlds, particularly for dynamic and interactable scenes. My goal is to develop highly realistic virtual spaces that enhance VR/AR immersive experiences, and improve training for embodied agents that can seamlessly generalize to the real world.
